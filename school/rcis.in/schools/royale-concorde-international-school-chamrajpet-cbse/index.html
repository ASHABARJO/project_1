About Royale Concorde International School, Chamrajpet, CBSE

Discover Royale Concorde International School (RCIS), a distinguished CBSE institution ranking among the top 10 schools in South Bangalore. With a network of campuses spanning both Bangalore and Mysore, we are delighted to announce the grand opening of our 8th branch in the vibrant locale of Chamrajpet.

Royale Concorde International School prides itself on its exceptional infrastructure, ensuring a conducive learning environment. Our commitment to safety is unwavering, as evidenced by our round-the-clock security monitoring system and reliable transportation services.

As an English medium co-educational day-school, Royale Concorde International School operates under the auspices of the esteemed Royale Concorde Educational Trust. We offer a comprehensive curriculum following the CBSE syllabus, coupled with innovative and student-centric teaching methodologies. Our affiliation with CBSE extends up to the secondary level, ensuring a seamless educational journey.

At Royale Concorde International School, our vision is clear: to emerge as a preeminent global educational institution nurturing the future leaders and visionaries of our nation and the world beyond. Join us in shaping a brighter tomorrow.

Curriculum

Pre-Primary Level PlaySchool, Nursery & KinderGarten

  • Pre-Primary: Cognitive skills, neatness, and language fluency.
  • Activities: field trips, arts, and crafts enhance creativity and motor skills.
  • Building self-confidence and social skills with Rhyme Time and Show & Tell

Primary Level Class 1 to IV

  • Fluency in spoken and written English through interactive teaching.
  • Mathematics: Develop number concepts, elementary geometry, and mental math skills.
  • Environmental Science: Learn science through hands-on activities, observation, and discovery.
  • Introduction to computers: Theory and practical experience for connected learning.
  • Holistic education: Embrace values, arts, crafts, and sports activities for well-rounded learning.

Middle and Secondary Level Class V to X

Class V to VIII

  • Accelerated academics, promoting questioning, hypothesizing, analysis, and creativity.
  • Follow CBSE's three-language policy, offering Hindi, Kannada & Sanskrit/French as 3rd languages.
  • Emphasis on teamwork through group discussions, projects, activities, and experimentation.
  • Hands-on learning of Math and Science concepts in dedicated labs.

Class IX to X

  • CBSE's integrated two-year curriculum for holistic assessment.
  • Focus on life skills, values, sports, and co-curricular activities.
  • Subjects include English, Hindi/Kannada, Math, General Science, Social Science.
  • Option for Artificial Intelligence as the 6th subject.
  • Two variants of Mathematics available for Grade X board exams.

Senior Secondary Level Class X

  • Senior Secondary: Two-year study program in Science, Commerce, and Humanities.
  • Science stream divided into Engineering and Medical branches. # Optional subjects for Engineering and Medical students.
  • Commerce stream offers optional subjects including Applied Mathematics and Legal Studies.
  • Humanities stream offers Legal Studies and Home Science.
  • Experienced faculty prepares students for professional learning.
  • Achieving excellence in Board exams, competitive exams, and professional placements.
