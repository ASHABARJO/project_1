Special Assemblies

World population Day

World population Day is an annual event, celebrated on 11th day of July to raise awareness of global population issues. The event was established by the governing council of the United Nations Development Programme . The day was celebrated with great enthusiasm by the students of St.Froebel School to mark the importance of the World Population Day.
The students expressed their deep concern and care for the healthy growth and safety of the human race. They showcased the consequences of population explosion that included food and water shortage and depletion of natural resources. The Principal was extremely contended to see the active participation and to hear the concern of young generation toward population explosion. she remarked that aim of conducting the special assembly is to spread awareness about the dire consequences of population explosion. She applauded efforts of the students and urged them to become good citizens and to contribute to the sustainable development through judicious use of natural resources.

World Chocolate Day

Chocolate Day is everyone's favourite day as everyone likes to get and gift the bunch of chocolates to their loved ones, friends et cetera chocolate Day celebration every year brings a favourite flavour of each one in their life. Tiny tots of St. Froebel School celebrated chocolate Day on 6th July 2022 by gifting chocolate to each other. The school was decorated with variety of chocolates like white chocolate, milk chocolate, ruby chocolate, dark chocolate etc.Children ate delicious chocolate and enjoyed the day with fun by watching chocolate making videos and other exciting and creative activities were also conducted.
Students brought their favourite chocolates and had a great time relishing them with their friends. Amidst a joyful celebration they also shared few lines on why they love chocolates and learnt some lip smacking chocolate recipes from the teachers.

Mother's Day

Mother's Day, also known as Mothering Sunday that honours family's or an individual's mother as well as maternal bonding and powerful impact of mothers on society.
Students of St. Froebel School observed Mother's Day to celebrate the everlasting bond of love and affection between a mother and her child. Students created art& craft items, greeting cards, wrote letters expressing gratitude and recited poems to pay respect to their mothers. Teachers also motivated and guided students in planning and preparing for this celebration. Teachers made an earnest effort to instil the importance of mother in a child's life through effective thematic presentation, moral stories, and video clippings. The tiny tots had a special celebration on Mother's Day. They intensely and devotedly spoke about their mothers. It was a pleasure watching the dynamic and energetic children preparing some beautiful cards on this special day. Each card was special and different in its own way.

Zoonoses Day

World Genesis Day is observed annually on July 6 to commemorate the scientific be breakthrough of the first Zoonotic disease vaccination. Zoonoses are the contagious illness brought on by viruses, germs and parasites that human contract from animals.
The students of St.Froebel School observed World Zoonoses day on Monday i;e 6th July to create awareness among the students and staff of the spread of zoonotic diseases to human beings. As a part of that event, quiz elocution, cartoon drawing, essay writing ,collage making etc were conducted for the students. The world Zoonosis day holds significance in the wake of virus Coronavirus disease pandemic believed to have been originated in bats and transmitted to humans via pangolins. Zoonoses is an infectious disease caused by a pathogen that originated in animals but the jumped to humans directly or through an intermediary species . Animals have therefore played an essential role in maintaining zoonotic infections – bacterial, viral or parasitic – in nature. COVID-19 disease caused by SARS – CoV-2 Virus is one such reality that plaguing the world today.

Maa tujhe salam

To mark the 75th Independence Day of India, St Froebel School, Paschim Vihar organised online celebration. The flag hoisting was done live within the school premises. An inter-house quiz titled '75 Years Of Independence' was organised for senior school students. A poetry recitation competition was also organised for students. Special Assembly on Gandhi Jayanti

Celebrating the birth anniversary of the father of the nation Mahatma Gandhi, students of St. Froebel School observed Gandhi Jayanti by organising a special assembly on 1 October 2021. The special assembly held was intended to paying heartfelt tribute to the great patriot and statesman Mahatma Gandhi. The assembly kicked off with Gandhiji's favourite bhajan Vaishnava Jana To by the school choir. Students recited poems depicting his sacrifice for the nation. A skit was presented by the primary school students. The skit presented had a deep impact on students as it reflected the attributes Gandhiji possessed i.e. truthfulness and honesty. The skit also recalled a visual metaphor of India's famous champion of peace Mahatma Gandhi, of the three vice monkeys representing the principles 'see on evil, hear no evil and speak no evil.' The Principal while addressing urged the students to stay away from vices such as lust, anger, greed and ego. She said all human souls are innately good but when man disconnects himself from his true nature, he lies, steals, cheats, covets and derides others. Truth and non-violence go hand in hand. This results in awakening of soul and helps us to live in complete harmony with fellow humans and nature.
