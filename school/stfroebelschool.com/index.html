WHO WE ARE

A story of commitment and preciseness

St. Froebel Senior Secondary School was established under the aegis of 'St. Froebel Education Society' in 1985. It is a professionally managed educational institution committed to establish new standards in the field of education. It is under the aegis of St. Froebel Education Society (Regd.), which was founded in April 1985 by a cohesive and like-minded team of educationists, having decades of experience in educational planning and administration both in India and Abroad. Aiming at imparting purposeful education to the wards of aspirant parents, the management has put into practice the message of Fredrich Froebel, the founder father of German Kinder Garten namely, "CHILD IS THE SEED IN THE GARDEN OF ENVIRONMENT".

SFS MASTER STROKE

St. Froebel School has been ranked number 1 amongst the most deserving schools in India by Education Today - one of the leading education portals. The school has been awarded the certificate of Achievement India's Top CBSE Schools parameter wise. The award was received by Mr. Yash Tyagi - the Director of the school on 8th December 2018. The school was felicitated amidst grand celebration in Bengaluru. The panel consisted of eminent personalities from education fraternity with a vision for excellence in education. The ranking considered various parameters like Academic Reputation, Co-curricular Education, Holistic Development, Sports Achievement, Infrastructure, Innovative Teaching, Special Need Education etc. The chairman of the school Mr. Sanjay Tyagi Ji congratulated the principal, the staff, students and parents on this remarkable achievement enjoined them to continue their efforts to make the school a centre of excellence. It is a glorifying and exalting moment for the entire SFS family.

SFS Bags Global Innovative School Award

SFS family is ecstatically thrilled and proud to share the news that SFS has been awarded with the prestigious and internationally acclaimed Global Innovative School Award 2020 for innovation in Pedagogy. The school director Ms. Sonia Nagpal received the award on 14 December at an award ceremony in Dubai, UAE. Many A-list educators and the most illustrious personalities from the associated field were present on this occasion. This is an annual award that identifies schools that have excelled in terms of scale of Academic Performance and Technological Infusion. The award has instilled in us a new elan and spirit to become one of the leading schools in the world. The Principal Dr. Ragini Kaul expressed her happiness on receiving the award and congratulated the staff and students for their contribution and unstinting support.

IN THE PURSUIT OF EXCELLENCE

It was indeed a matter of great pride for all froebelians to be awarded with the prestigious NATIONAL TEACHING EXCELLENCE AWARD 2018 by the board of Governors of Educational Standard and Testing Council of India and Confederation of Indian Universities. The award was conferred upon St. Froebel School. Its Chairperson Mr. Sanjay Tyagi, the Principal and five Teachers Ms. Ranjana Sehgal, Ms. Mita Bose, Ms. Bhanu Vij, Ms. Anshu Bansal and Ms. Inderpreet Kaur and seven students Saanvi Mishra, Nikita Bhutani, Parul Sharma, Nancy, Yashika Bansiwal, Pratishtha Monga and Yashika Malhotra by Dr. P R Trivedi, President Patron, CIU in the presence of Chief Guest, Honourable, Governor of Tripura Prof. Kaptan Singh Solanki and Venezuela's Ambassador Augusto Montiel. Shortlisted schools were presented the award. St. Froebel is constantly striving for perfection and excellence infusing life and relevance into the education experience of every child. This brilliant achievement will inspire the school to achieve many more milestones in future. It will continue to play a cardinal role in building up of the character of the next generation, kindling the young minds to aspire.
